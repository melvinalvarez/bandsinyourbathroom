About

Are you tired of running ads on Facebook to people who don’t care about your music? Do you spend countless hours working on marketing your music but seeing little to no return? Wouldn’t you want to advertise directly to someone listening to your type of music, and paying to go to shows?

That’s what Bands In Your Bathroom is here to do.

Bands In Your Bathroom is an advertising service used for marketing bands and musicians inside the bathrooms of music venues. These ads go above urinals, and inside bathroom stalls for maximum exposure.

What better way to advertise your music than to put it directly in front of a music listeners face? Each ad is an engaging 11x17 frame including a promo picture, social media info, new music info, and something saying “let us know what bathroom you found us(me) in!” so the person seeing the ad might actually engage on social media.

We are in venues that have new shows everyday, so there are new people seeing the ads everyday, and there are thousands and thousands of people seeing it per month. We have ads all over the country, and with ads starting as low as $20, you can start advertising in bathrooms now!
